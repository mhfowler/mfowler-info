Looking Glass

2017

installation, projection mapping, open frameworks

Looking Glass was an installation exhibited at Acud Macht Neue in Berlin for a School Of Machines community show.

For the installation I created 3 minutes of animations using the vector shapes of a broken mirror as input.

Pariefracture, a word coined by Dmitry Vagner, is the moment when an unknown unknown becomes known. When something you didn't know suprises you and a previous perspective is shattered. When you walk into the screen door.

Looking Glass is a tribute to pariefracture and it's role in expanding empathy for others beyond the limits of our understanding.
