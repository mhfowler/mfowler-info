2 pots

August 2016

openframeworks, arduino, potentiometers

2 pots was an installation created for the SFPC final show at the end of a 10 week session studying code, design, hardware and theory.

Using a handmade cardboard interface housing an arduino and 2 potentiometers (2 pots), visitors could control parameters of a series of generative line drawings exploring controlled noise.
