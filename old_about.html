Max Fowler is a freelance programmer and artist based in New York.

Some of my recent projects have explored the ways we conceptualize space, expressive analysis of text message history, and how to use a twitter bot to take out the trash.

Max Fowler codes, makes generative art, speculative startups, twitter bots and vaporware.

I'm interested in using data visualization to explore subjectivity, generative systems, and the ways we use technology to decide what to read.
